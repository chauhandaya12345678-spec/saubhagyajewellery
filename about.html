OUR STORY

About Us

Saubhagya is a Mumbai manufacturer of handcrafted premium imitation jewellery — for weddings, festivals and every day in between. We make what we sell and ship it insured across India.

The Workshop

We pair traditional Indian craftsmanship with modern CAD design. Every piece is modelled in Rhino, hand-finished in matte antique gold or high-shine polish, and individually inspected before it leaves our Mumbai warehouse.

Our mission is simple: high-quality artificial jewellery that looks and feels like fine jewellery, at a fraction of the price, so you can wear something extraordinary to every occasion.

Manufacturer & Direct Seller

We are the manufacturer of every piece on this site — there is no marketplace or reseller between you and us. That means better prices, tighter quality control and a single point of contact for every order.

Our Promise

Free insured shipping across India and a quality guarantee on every handcrafted piece. Country of origin for every product: India.
