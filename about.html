OUR STORY

About Us

Saubhagya is a Mumbai atelier crafting premium designer imitation jewellery: temple, Kundan/Polki and American Diamond, for weddings, festivals and every day in between.

The House

Born in the lanes of Zaveri Bazaar, we pair traditional Indian craftsmanship with modern CAD design. Every piece is modelled in Rhino, hand-finished in matte antique gold or high-shine AD, and individually inspected before it leaves the atelier.

Our mission is simple: high-quality artificial jewellery that looks and feels like fine jewellery, at a fraction of the price, so you can wear something extraordinary to every occasion.

Our Promise

Free insured shipping across India, GST included on every invoice, and a bespoke quality guarantee on every handcrafted piece.
