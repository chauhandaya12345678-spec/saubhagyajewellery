How to raise a concern

Email or WhatsApp us with your order number and a short description of the issue. For manufacturing-defect claims please attach your continuous unboxing video (see standards page) within 48 hours of delivery.

Our SLA

  • Acknowledgement: within 48 hours of receipt.
  • Resolution: within 30 calendar days (typically 7 working days).
  • Escalation: if unresolved after 30 days, you may escalate to the National Consumer Helpline (1800-11-4000) or file a complaint at consumerhelpline.gov.in.
