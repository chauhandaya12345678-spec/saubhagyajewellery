Data we collect & purpose

Legal basis

We rely on your explicit consent (given at the cookie banner and sign-up/checkout checkboxes) and on legitimate performance of a contract you initiate (your order). Marketing emails are sent only if you tick the marketing-consent box.

Sharing

Order data is shared with Shiprocket (logistics), Razorpay (payment) and Resend (transactional email) strictly to complete your order. We do not sell your data or share it for third-party marketing.

Retention

Account and order records are kept for 7 years for tax and audit compliance. Marketing-consent flag is kept until you unsubscribe.

Your rights (DPDPA)

You have the right to access, correct, update or erase your data, withdraw consent, and to nominate someone to exercise these rights on your behalf. Write to care@saubhagyajewellery.com; we respond within 30 days.
