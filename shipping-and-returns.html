Shipping & Returns

Free insured shipping across India on every order.

Shipping & Delivery

  • Ready pieces: dispatch within 2 – 4 business days.
  • Made-to-order bridal sets: dispatch within 10 – 14 business days.
  • Delivery ETA: Mumbai 2 – 4 days · Maharashtra 3 – 5 days · rest of India 5 – 8 days after dispatch.
  • Free insured shipping across India on every order. Country of origin: India (Made in Mumbai).

No-Return Policy (change of mind)

ALL SALES ARE FINAL. Every piece is made-to-order and individually inspected before dispatch; we do not accept returns, exchanges or cancellations for change of mind. This protects every client from receiving a previously worn piece.

Defect Claim Window

Manufacturing defects claimed within 48 hours of delivery with a continuous unboxing video are repaired or replaced free of cost. Beyond 48 hours, defect claims cannot be accepted. Refunds for verified defects (when repair or replacement is not possible) are processed to the original payment method within 7 – 10 business days.
