Shipping & Returns

Free insured shipping across India on every order.

Shipping & Delivery

Ready pieces dispatch within 2 to 4 business days; made-to-order bridal sets within 10 to 14 days. Every shipment is fully insured in transit and delivered across India.

No-Return Policy

ALL SALES ARE FINAL. Because every piece is made-to-order and individually inspected before dispatch, we do not accept returns, exchanges or cancellations for change of mind — no exceptions. This protects every client from receiving a previously worn piece.

The only exception: a verified manufacturing defect, which we repair or replace free of cost. Defect claims require the continuous unboxing video described in our standards page, reported within 48 hours of delivery.
